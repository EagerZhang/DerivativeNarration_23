“Derivative Narration” is a project that seeks posibility of creative graphic making with machine-generated literature.

The workshop was first made possible with Graphic Design MFA students at Otis College of Art and Design, as part of the Otis GD MFA Art Book Fair, Summer 2023.
Font In Use
The font family in use is Korpus Grotesk, distributed by Binnenland Typefaces.
